I do not like Django

12 Jul 2017 .. 2 min
django / flask

First of all, I do not know why, it is just my feeling. Two years ago I had to build small internal company tool for data administration. That was my first serious web application. I have read about different options, asked about best practices and built ‘hello world apps’ in flask, django and pyramid. Most advices I got was to use django (best choice for beginners), but it just did not work for me. Two years later I have not stop developing using Flask, my app grown bigger but still does its job quite good, though I have rewritten it twice..

Having said that, I am learning Django now. The reason is simply current job market in my city. Most Python jobs are Django related. So I am slowly going through official tutorial, and have few other good books waiting to be consumed (see resources at the end of this post).

Looking on my developer career I feel that is not last choice..

Ps.

Flask is finally updating regularly (it was stuck with version 0.11 for years)

Checkout new Pyramid website ;)

And build your own framework better than Django/Flask..
